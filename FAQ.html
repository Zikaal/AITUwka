What is this social network and who created it?
Our social network was created by three university students to simplify interactions between students, professors, and staff.
What features are available on this social network?
You can create groups, save important posts, chat with friends, share information, and participate in discussions.
How do I register?
You can register using your university email and create a unique profile with your personal information.
Can I join multiple groups?
Yes, you can be a member of several groups related to your interests, studies, or extracurricular activities.
How can I find my friends?
You can use the search bar to find users by name, faculty, or interests.
What should I do if I forget my password?
On the login page, select "Forgot password?" and follow the instructions to reset it.
